Buttermiksoup

Description

General recipe for a buttermilksoup.

Ingredients

  • 2l buttermilk
  • lemon skin
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 30-40g Maizena
  • sugar
  • vanilla sugar

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients exept the sugar.
  2. Get it to boil and season it with the sugar.
  3. If you want you can serve the soup with semolina dumplings.
